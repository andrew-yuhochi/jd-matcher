=== CAN HIRE IN CANADA ===
Assess whether this employer can hire a candidate who is physically located
in Canada. Pick EXACTLY ONE of: yes | likely | no | unclear

Definitions:
  yes     = Canadian-domiciled employer OR explicit Canadian subsidiary OR JD
            explicitly states "we hire in Canada" / "this role is open to
            Canadian residents". Canadian company is sufficient for "yes".
  likely  = US or global employer with known Canadian engineering presence
            (Vancouver, Toronto, Montreal offices well-known) even if not
            stated explicitly for THIS role.
  no      = US-only employer that explicitly excludes Canadian residents, or
            EU-only employer, or JD states "must be located in [US city]" with
            no Canada mention.
  unclear = JD provides no geographic signal either way.

Examples:
  Shopify (Ottawa HQ) → "yes"
  TD Bank, RBC, Manulife → "yes"
  Google (posted for Mountain View office, no Canada mention) → "likely"
  (Google has major Canadian engineering presence in Waterloo/Montreal/Toronto)
  A US startup with no stated Canadian presence → "unclear"
  "Must be located in Seattle, WA" with no Canada mention → "no"
  Government of BC → "yes"
